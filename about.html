About Sunspectrum Enterprises

Sunspectrum Enterprises is a Karnataka-based water and energy solutions provider serving residential, commercial, and agricultural customers across the state. We specialize in Solar Water Heaters, RO Plants, Heat Pumps, Water Softeners, Solar Panels, Purifiers, and Solar Pump Sets.

We supply and install high-quality systems including Saurdarshan Solar – Glass Line Solar Water Heaters, designed for durability, hard-water resistance, and long service life.

Our services cover consultation, installation, and after-sales support for homes, apartments, hotels, and commercial setups throughout Karnataka.

With extensive field experience across the state, we help customers reduce electricity usage while improving hot-water availability and water quality.

Our Mission

To provide reliable solar and water solutions that help homes and businesses across Karnataka reduce electricity costs while improving water quality through dependable products and professional service.
