Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you provide?
We provide RO plants, solar water heating systems, solar panels, heat pumps, water softeners, and complete installation & maintenance services for all products.
Do you offer site visits before installation?
Yes — we inspect your location to understand your requirements and suggest the most suitable system. Site visits are free within our service area.
Is installation included in the price?
Yes, standard installation is included in the quoted price for all products. Additional civil work or special requirements may be charged separately.
Do products come with warranty?
Yes — manufacturer warranty applies on all products. Typical warranties: Solar products (5-10 years), RO systems (1-2 years), Heat Pumps (2-5 years).
How long does installation take?
RO systems: 2–4 hours. Solar water heaters: usually 1–2 days depending on system size and complexity. Commercial projects may take longer.
Do you provide annual maintenance contracts (AMC)?
Yes — we offer AMC plans for all our products including regular servicing, priority support, and discounted repairs.
Which areas do you serve?
We serve customers across Karnataka including Bengaluru, Mysore, Bagalkote, Hubli, Mangalore, and all major districts. Contact us for service anywhere in the state.
How can I book a service or request a quote?
You can reach us by phone, WhatsApp, or email. Click the contact buttons below or use the WhatsApp floating button to chat with us instantly.
Do you provide emergency service?
Yes — we offer emergency repair services for critical systems. Please call us directly for urgent requirements.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept bank transfers, UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), credit/debit cards, and cash. For large projects, installment options may be available.
